Navigating life is hardly linear; it sometimes comes with many twists and turns that leave us feeling anxious, stuck, depressed and/or overwhelmed. In these moments, we feel confused and demotivated, alone with our thoughts, feelings and emotions. Alone, you may find it more difficult to process these situations fully. I am here to create a warm, safe, compassionate and non-judgemental space to help you to unburden your thoughts, feelings and emotions. I have lived on three continents – Africa, Europe and North America- and I appreciate our unique cultures and individual experiences. I have experienced many life stressors that immigrants and multicultural groups face, such as acculturation, discrimination, stigmatization and racism. These experiences have given me a deeper understanding of the intersectional barriers and mental health challenges people from different socio-economic and cultural backgrounds go through. My priority is to build a trusting relationship with you and empower you with the tools and strategies you need to bring positive change. My therapeutic approach is integrative as I draw from solutions and emotionally therapeutic frameworks, leveraging clients’ diverse experiences and individuality, mutual respect, and cultural sensitivity.
